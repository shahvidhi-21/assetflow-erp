Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Project Name

AssetFlow – Enterprise Asset & Resource Management System

1. Project Overview

AssetFlow is a web-based Enterprise Asset & Resource Management System that enables organizations to manage physical assets and shared resources from a centralized platform.

Organizations often use spreadsheets to track laptops, furniture, projectors, meeting rooms, vehicles, and other assets. This leads to duplicate allocations, missing assets, scheduling conflicts, and poor maintenance tracking.

AssetFlow solves these problems by providing role-based asset management, allocation, booking, maintenance workflows, and reporting.

2. Objective

Develop a scalable full-stack web application where organizations can:

Manage departments and employees
Register company assets
Track the complete lifecycle of every asset
Allocate assets to employees
Prevent duplicate allocations
Book shared resources without scheduling conflicts
Manage maintenance requests
View dashboards and reports
Maintain activity logs
